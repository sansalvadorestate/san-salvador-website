The Conference Room is a small, fully equipped *meeting room* with a *front and back porch*. Built with greenheart, the glass windows give a perfect view of the natural surroundings. It contains the following amenities:
- Projector
- Projector Screen
- WiFi
- White-board with paper and markers
- Air-conditioning
- Refrigerator
- Microwave
- Kettle & Sink
- A unisex Washroom

It is a board room style setting, seating up to `16` persons *around a rectangular table*. There is additional seating for up to `20` persons. 

> It is ideal for **meetings**, **seminars**, **retreats**, **team builders**.

> It can also be booked for **weddings**.
